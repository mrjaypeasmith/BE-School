About

We are a team of native English speakers in Mexico City. We love to teach face-to-face, using modern techniques. Our goal is to help entrepreneurs and business professionals exceed their potential, helping them connect and do business globally. Read our Mission Statement.

Method

Our method offers a combination of theory, practice, dramatization, role-play, and technology in order to increase self-awareness and focus on negotiations. This allows participants to apply the skills acquired in real world situations.

Our Mission

be School is a premiere learning institute for business professionals seeking to gain confidence in their English language skills. We are committed to teaching professionals how to effectively communicate in the workplace, using English in both verbal and written form.

Our objective is to immerse Mexican business professionals in daily activities that will build confidence in their English language development, regardless of situation or context. Our students will engage with their English colleagues with poise and conviction.

We value each customer as an individual, not a level or a number. We take pride in our personalised approach, bespoke materials, and engaging teaching style. be School will identify the primary language needs of the student to ensure appropriate tutelage.

Changing the way that Business English schools operate in Mexico, be School provides a holistic approach to learning. This approach encourages student achievement and creates further opportunities for career growth within the international business community.

be School is committed to building trust between our partnerships, working alongside our partnership companies, and developing global Ambassadors.
